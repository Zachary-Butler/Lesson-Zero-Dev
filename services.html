FAQ

Common questions

What's the timeline for each tier?

Bootstrap typically delivers in 2-3 weeks, Launch in 3-4 weeks, and Flagship in 4-6 weeks depending on travel and interview scheduling. Rush delivery available for additional fee.

How many revisions are included?

All tiers include two rounds of revisions on the edited video. Additional revisions can be purchased if needed. We work collaboratively to get it right.

What if I need something custom?

These packages are starting points. Every EdTech company has unique needs - we're happy to customize services, combine elements from different tiers, or create entirely custom proposals.

Do you provide the script?

Yes! All tiers include scriptwriting as part of the consultation and planning process. We collaborate with you to develop messaging that resonates with educators.

What video formats do you deliver?

We deliver multiple formats optimized for different platforms - full resolution master files, web-optimized versions, social media cuts, and any custom formats you need.

Can you help with video strategy?

Absolutely. With our background in both filmmaking and education, we can advise on video strategy, distribution, and how to maximize impact with your educator audience.
