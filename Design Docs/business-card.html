Exporting for print (Moo.com)

  1. Open this file in Chrome (fonts load via CDN)
  2. Ctrl+P → set Destination to "Save as PDF" (Chrome's built-in, not Adobe PDF) → More settings → check Background graphics → Save
  3. The PDF will have 2 pages at 3.75×2.25" (includes 0.125" bleed): page 1 = front, page 2 = back
  4. On moo.com → Business Cards → Upload Your Own Design → upload the PDF → do not shrink to fit → choose Luxe or Super stock
